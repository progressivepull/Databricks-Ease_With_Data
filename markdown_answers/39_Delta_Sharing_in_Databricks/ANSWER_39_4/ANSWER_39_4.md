# ANSWER 39 4

**Question 4**

**Which of the following is not one of the four primary Delta Sharing components?**

**A. Provider ❌ Incorrect**

Provider is one of the core components.

**B. Share ❌ Incorrect**

A Share is the container holding shared objects.

**C. Recipient ❌ Incorrect**

Recipients consume shared data.

**D. Catalog ❌ Incorrect**

On the recipient side, shared objects are exposed through a Delta Sharing Catalog in Unity Catalog.

**E. Checkpoint ✅ Correct**

Checkpoints belong to:

- Structured Streaming

- Auto Loader

They have nothing to do with Delta Sharing.

**The four main components**

Provider

↓

Share

↓

Recipient

↓

Catalog

**Why E is correct**

Checkpoints are streaming metadata, not Delta Sharing components.
