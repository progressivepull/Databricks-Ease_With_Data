# ANSWER 47 6

**Question 6**

**Why would you add custom instructions to a Genie Space?**

**A. To change Unity Catalog permissions automatically** ❌ Incorrect

- Custom instructions don't affect security.

**B. To teach Genie organization-specific terminology, abbreviations, and business language** ✅ Correct

Every company has unique vocabulary.

Examples

"LOB"

Could mean:

Line of Business

"VIP"

Could mean:

Preferred Customer

"EDP"

Could mean:

Enterprise Data Platform

Custom instructions teach Genie these meanings.

Result:

Much better answers.

**C. To create SQL Warehouses automatically** ❌ Incorrect

- Warehouses are created separately.

**D. To replace the need for metadata descriptions** ❌ Incorrect

- Metadata is still extremely important.

**E. To schedule dashboard refreshes** ❌ Incorrect

- Dashboard scheduling is unrelated.

**Key Concept**

**Custom Instructions = Teach Genie your company's language.**
