# ANSWER 02 2

**What is the primary goal of Artificial Intelligence (AI)?**

A. To permanently replace all computer hardware with human workers\
B. To store data in spreadsheets without performing analysis\
C. To enable machines and software systems to simulate human intelligence and perform tasks such as learning, reasoning, and decision-making\
D. To convert all structured databases into image files for visualization\
E. To physically increase internet speed by upgrading network cables automatically

**Correct Answer: C. To enable machines and software systems to simulate human intelligence and perform tasks such as learning, reasoning, and decision-making**

**Breakdown of Each Answer Option**

**A. To permanently replace all computer hardware with human workers ❌ Wrong**

AI is software-based intelligence, not a replacement for hardware.

AI runs *on* hardware such as:

- CPUs

- GPUs

- servers

- cloud infrastructure

Also, AI is designed to automate or assist tasks, not replace computer hardware with humans.

This option confuses:

- hardware infrastructure\
  with

- intelligent software systems.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**B. To store data in spreadsheets without performing analysis ❌ Wrong**

AI is not primarily a data storage technology.

Spreadsheets are used for:

- organizing data

- manual calculations

- reporting

AI focuses on:

- learning from data

- pattern recognition

- predictions

- reasoning

- automation

Simply storing data without analysis is not intelligence.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**C. To enable machines and software systems to simulate human intelligence and perform tasks such as learning, reasoning, and decision-making ✅ Correct**

This is the correct definition of AI.

Artificial Intelligence aims to create systems that can perform tasks typically requiring human intelligence, including:

- learning from data

- recognizing patterns

- understanding language

- making decisions

- solving problems

- generating content

Examples:

- ChatGPT

- recommendation systems

- self-driving cars

- fraud detection

- virtual assistants

AI simulates aspects of human cognitive abilities.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**D. To convert all structured databases into image files for visualization ❌ Wrong**

AI is not designed to transform databases into images.

While AI can help with:

- computer vision

- image generation

- visualization

converting structured databases into images is not the core purpose of AI.

Databases are usually analyzed using:

- SQL

- BI tools

- analytics platforms

not converted into image files.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**E. To physically increase internet speed by upgrading network cables automatically ❌ Wrong**

AI does not physically upgrade network hardware.

Internet speed improvements usually involve:

- better networking infrastructure

- higher bandwidth

- upgraded routers/cables

- telecom engineering

AI may help optimize network traffic or predict failures, but it does not directly increase internet speed by replacing cables.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Key Concept to Remember**

The central goal of Artificial Intelligence is:

**Creating systems that can mimic aspects of human intelligence such as learning, reasoning, understanding, and decision-making.**

AI is fundamentally about:

- intelligence

- automation

- learning from data

- making smart decisions

—not hardware replacement, storage, or networking upgrades.
