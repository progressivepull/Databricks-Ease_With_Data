# ANSWER 02 4

**What is the primary responsibility of a Data Engineer in a modern data platform?**

A. To design marketing advertisements and social media campaigns for customers\
B. To build and maintain data pipelines, storage systems, and data infrastructure for processing and analytics\
C. To manually create every business report using spreadsheet formulas only\
D. To physically install networking cables and replace computer hardware in data centers\
E. To focus exclusively on graphic design and website color themes for dashboards

**Correct Answer: B. To build and maintain data pipelines, storage systems, and data infrastructure for processing and analytics**

**Breakdown of Each Answer Option**

**A. To design marketing advertisements and social media campaigns for customers ❌ Wrong**

This is primarily the responsibility of:

- marketing teams

- digital media specialists

- advertising professionals

Data Engineers may support marketing teams by preparing customer or campaign data, but they do not design advertisements themselves.

Their role is technical and infrastructure-focused.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**B. To build and maintain data pipelines, storage systems, and data infrastructure for processing and analytics ✅ Correct**

This is the core responsibility of a Data Engineer.

Data Engineers build systems that:

- collect data

- transform data

- move data

- store data

- prepare data for analytics and AI

Common responsibilities include:

- ETL/ELT pipelines

- Spark jobs

- data lake/lakehouse management

- streaming pipelines

- data quality

- orchestration workflows

Tools often used:

- Databricks

- Apache Spark

- Airflow

- Kafka

- SQL

- Delta Lake

They create the foundation that analysts, scientists, and BI teams depend on.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**C. To manually create every business report using spreadsheet formulas only ❌ Wrong**

Creating business reports is usually handled by:

- Business Analysts
