# ANSWER 05 10

**Question 10**

**Why does the Databricks Account Console become increasingly valuable in large organizations?**

**A. It automatically creates Spark applications.** ❌ Incorrect

- Developers build Spark applications.

- The Account Console does not generate them.

**B. It eliminates the need for Unity Catalog.** ❌ Incorrect

- Unity Catalog remains essential for governance.

- The Account Console works alongside it.

**C. It centralizes management of users, workspaces, permissions, networking, and account-wide settings.** ✅ **Correct**

- Large enterprises often have:

  - Multiple workspaces

  - Hundreds or thousands of users

  - Numerous groups

  - Multiple cloud resources

- The Account Console provides one centralized place to manage these resources efficiently.

**D. It replaces Azure Portal administration.** ❌ Incorrect

- Azure Portal still manages Azure resources such as subscriptions, VNets, storage accounts, and resource groups.

- The Account Console manages Databricks-specific resources.

**E. It removes the need for Service Principals.** ❌ Incorrect

- Automation still requires Service Principals.

- The Account Console manages them but does not eliminate their need.

**Key Concept**

As organizations grow, centralized administration becomes essential. The Databricks Account Console serves as the **single control center** for managing the entire Databricks environment, improving scalability, governance, security, and operational efficiency.
