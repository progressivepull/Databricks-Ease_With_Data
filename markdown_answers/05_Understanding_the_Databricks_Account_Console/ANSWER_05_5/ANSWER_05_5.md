# ANSWER 05 5

**Question 5**

**Who has permission to manage users through the Databricks Account Console?**

**A. Workspace Administrators** ❌ Incorrect

- Workspace Administrators manage users only within a specific workspace.

- They cannot manage the entire account.

**B. Metastore Administrators** ❌ Incorrect

- They manage Unity Catalog objects and permissions.

- They do not manage account users.

**C. Service Principals** ❌ Incorrect

- Service Principals are identities used by applications.

- They are not administrators by default.

**D. Account Administrators** ✅ **Correct**

- Account Administrators control:

  - Users

  - Groups

  - Service Principals

  - Workspaces

  - Networking

  - Account-wide settings

**E. Catalog Owners** ❌ Incorrect

- Catalog Owners manage specific catalogs.

- They do not administer users for the account.

**Key Concept**

Account Administrator is the **highest administrative role** within a Databricks account.
