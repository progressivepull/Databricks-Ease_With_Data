# ANSWER 05 9

**Question 9**

**Which feature can be enabled at the account level through the Settings section?**

**A. Delta Time Travel** ❌ Incorrect

- Time Travel is a Delta Lake table feature.

- It is not enabled from the Account Console.

**B. Photon Acceleration** ❌ Incorrect

- Photon is enabled on supported compute resources.

- It is not an account-level setting in the Account Console.

**C. Serverless Compute for Workflows, Notebooks, and Delta Live Tables (DLT)** ✅ **Correct**

- Administrators can enable serverless capabilities at the account level.

- Serverless reduces infrastructure management and automatically provisions compute for supported workloads.

**D. Auto Loader** ❌ Incorrect

- Auto Loader is a data ingestion feature configured in notebooks or pipelines.

**E. Liquid Clustering** ❌ Incorrect

- Liquid Clustering is a Delta Lake table optimization feature.

- It is not enabled from the Account Console Settings.

**Key Concept**

The **Settings** section controls **account-wide capabilities**, such as Serverless Compute.
