# ANSWER 10 9

**Question 9**

**Who automatically becomes the Metastore Administrator after creating the Metastore?**

**A. Every Workspace Administrator** ❌ Incorrect

- Workspace Administrators manage workspaces.

- They do not automatically become Metastore Administrators.

**B. The user who created the Metastore** ✅ Correct

The creator automatically becomes the Metastore Administrator.

They can:

- Create catalogs

- Assign permissions

- Delegate admin rights

- Configure governance

**C. All users in the Databricks account** ❌ Incorrect

- That would be a major security risk.

**D. The Azure Subscription Owner only** ❌ Incorrect

- Azure ownership does not automatically grant Unity Catalog administration.

**E. Every Service Principal** ❌ Incorrect

- Service Principals only receive permissions if explicitly granted.

**Key Concept**

Creator = First Metastore Administrator.
