# ANSWER 40 7

**Question 7**

**What is the primary purpose of a Budget Policy in Serverless Compute?**

**A. To encrypt notebook code automatically** ❌ Incorrect

- Encryption is unrelated.

**B. To schedule notebook execution** ❌ Incorrect

- Scheduling belongs to Workflows/Jobs.

**C. To tag, monitor, and allocate Serverless DBU costs for reporting and chargeback** ✅ Correct

- Budget Policies help organizations:

  - Track spending

  - Allocate costs

  - Report usage

  - Perform chargeback/showback

**D. To determine Spark executor memory settings** ❌ Incorrect

- Budget Policies don't configure Spark.

**E. To create Unity Catalog schemas automatically** ❌ Incorrect

- Budget Policies aren't governance tools.

**Key Concept**

Budget Policies = cost tracking.
