# ANSWER 40 9

**Question 9**

**How is Serverless Compute billed?**

**A. Separate cloud virtual machine charges plus Databricks DBUs** ❌ Incorrect

- That's how Classic Compute is commonly billed.

- With Serverless, infrastructure costs are bundled.

**B. Fixed monthly subscription regardless of usage** ❌ Incorrect

- Billing is usage-based.

**C. Databricks DBUs only, with infrastructure and virtual machine costs included** ✅ Correct

- You pay only Serverless DBUs.

- VM and infrastructure costs are already included.

**D. Virtual machine charges only** ❌ Incorrect

- VM costs are not billed separately.

**E. One-time licensing fee per workspace** ❌ Incorrect

- Databricks does not use a one-time workspace license.

**Key Concept**

Classic Compute:

Cloud VM Cost

\+ Databricks DBUs

Serverless:

Serverless DBUs

(includes infrastructure)
