# ANSWER 40 5

**Question 5**

**What happens when a Serverless Compute workload requires additional resources?**

**A. Users must manually resize the cluster.** ❌ Incorrect

- Manual resizing belongs to Classic Compute.

**B. The workload stops until an administrator adds more virtual machines.** ❌ Incorrect

- Scaling is automatic.

**C. Serverless automatically scales resources up or down as needed.** ✅ Correct

- Databricks continuously adjusts resources.

- More executors when workload increases.

- Fewer when workload decreases.

**D. The workload is moved to a Classic Compute cluster.** ❌ Incorrect

- Serverless remains Serverless.

**E. Spark executors are permanently fixed after startup.** ❌ Incorrect

- Executors change dynamically.

**Key Concept**

Serverless provides elastic scaling.
