# ANSWER 19 4

**Question 4**

**Which two conditions are used by the child notebook to filter employee data?**

**A. Department and Employee ID** ❌ Incorrect

- Employee ID uniquely identifies employees.

- It wasn't part of the lesson's filtering logic.

**B. Salary and Department** ❌ Incorrect

- Salary wasn't one of the filtering conditions.

**C. Department parameter and Active Record = 1** ✅ Correct

The child notebook filters using:

1.  The department supplied by the parent notebook.

2.  Active employees only.

Example:

Department = Sales\
\
AND\
\
Active_Record = 1

Only active Sales employees are processed.

**D. Employee Name and Department** ❌ Incorrect

- Employee names were not used.

**E. Department and Hire Date** ❌ Incorrect

- Hire date wasn't used.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Memory Trick**

Filter:

Department\
\
+\
\
Active = 1
