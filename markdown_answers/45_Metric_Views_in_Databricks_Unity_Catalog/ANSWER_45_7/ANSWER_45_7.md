# ANSWER 45 7

**Question 7**

**What advanced capability allows Metric Views to calculate values such as Current Year Orders and Previous Year Orders?**

**A. Delta Sharing** ❌ Incorrect

- Delta Sharing shares data.

**B. Window-based measures** ✅ Correct

Window-based measures perform calculations across rows.

Examples:

- Current Year Sales

- Previous Year Sales

- Running Total

- Moving Average

- Year-over-Year Growth

Example

2024 Orders

2025 Orders

Compare

This comparison is enabled through window-based measures.

**C. Streaming Tables** ❌ Incorrect

- Streaming Tables ingest data.

**D. Foreign Catalogs** ❌ Incorrect

- Foreign Catalogs connect to external databases.

**E. SQL Alerts** ❌ Incorrect

- Alerts monitor data.

**Key Concept**

**Window Measures = Time-based comparisons and advanced analytics.**
