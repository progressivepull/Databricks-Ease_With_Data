# ANSWER 45 2

**Question 2**

**Which two types of objects are defined within a Metric View?**

**A. Catalogs and Schemas** ❌ Incorrect

- Catalogs and Schemas organize data.

- They are not Metric View components.

**B. Pipelines and Jobs** ❌ Incorrect

- Pipelines process data.

- Jobs schedule execution.

**C. Dimensions and Measures** ✅ Correct

A Metric View contains:

**Dimensions**

Used for grouping.

Examples:

- Customer

- Region

- Product

- Year

- Order Status

**Measures**

Used for calculations.

Examples:

- Revenue

- Sales

- Total Orders

- Average Price

**D. Clusters and Warehouses** ❌ Incorrect

- Compute resources are not Metric View objects.

**E. Volumes and Connections** ❌ Incorrect

- These relate to storage and Lakehouse Federation.

**Key Concept**

Think:

**Dimensions = "Slice the data"**

**Measures = "Calculate the data"**
