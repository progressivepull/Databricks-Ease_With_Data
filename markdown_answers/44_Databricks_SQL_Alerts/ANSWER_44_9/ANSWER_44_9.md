# ANSWER 44 9

**Question 9**

**Who can create notification destinations such as Email, Slack, or Microsoft Teams in Databricks?**

**A. Any workspace user** ❌ Incorrect

- Notification destinations affect the entire workspace.

- They require administrative privileges.

**B. Catalog Owners only** ❌ Incorrect

- Catalog Owners manage data objects, not workspace notifications.

**C. Workspace Administrators** ✅ Correct

- Workspace Admins configure:

  - Email destinations

  - Slack webhooks

  - Teams integrations

  - PagerDuty integrations

**D. SQL Analysts only** ❌ Incorrect

- Analysts can use destinations but typically cannot create workspace-wide notification destinations.

**E. Metastore Administrators only** ❌ Incorrect

- Metastore Admins govern Unity Catalog metadata, not workspace notification settings.

**Key Concept**

**Workspace Admins configure notification destinations.**
