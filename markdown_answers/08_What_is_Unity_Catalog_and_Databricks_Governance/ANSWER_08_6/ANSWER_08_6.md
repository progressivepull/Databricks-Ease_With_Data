# ANSWER 08 6

**Question 6**

**Which of the following objects can be stored inside a schema?**

**A. Virtual Networks and Clusters** ❌ Incorrect

- These are compute and networking resources.

- Not database objects.

**B. Tables, Views, Functions, and Volumes** ✅ Correct

A schema organizes data objects like:

- Tables

- Views

- Functions

- Volumes

Think of a schema as a folder.

**C. Workspaces and Users** ❌ Incorrect

- Workspaces and users exist outside schemas.

**D. Metastores and Catalogs** ❌ Incorrect

- These are above schemas.

**E. Storage Credentials and External Locations** ❌ Incorrect

- These are governance objects, not schema contents.

Hierarchy:

Catalog\
└── Schema\
├── Table\
├── View\
├── Function\
└── Volume
