# EXPLANATION 32 9

**Question 9**

**What is the primary purpose of Access Control Lists (ACLs) on Secret Scopes?**

**Correct Answer:** **C. To determine who can use, read, and manage Secret Scopes**

**Explanation**

ACLs define user and group permissions for Secret Scopes. Permissions typically include:

- **READ**

- **WRITE**

- **MANAGE**

This ensures only authorized users can access or administer secrets.

**Why the Correct Answer is Correct**

ACLs enforce security by controlling who can read, modify, or manage Secret Scopes.

**Why the Other Answers Are Wrong**

Other options confuse ACLs with encryption or storage mechanisms.
