# EXPLANATION 26 7

**Question 7**

**In the Medallion Architecture demonstrated in the lesson, what is the typical order of data refinement?**

**Correct Answer:** **D. Bronze → Silver → Gold**

**Explanation**

**Bronze**

- Raw ingestion

- Minimal processing

↓

**Silver**

- Cleaned

- Validated

- Joined

- Standardized

↓

**Gold**

- Aggregated

- Business-ready

- Analytics-ready

**Why the Correct Answer is Correct**

Each layer increases data quality and business value.

**Why the Other Answers Are Wrong**

They either skip layers or reverse the refinement process.
