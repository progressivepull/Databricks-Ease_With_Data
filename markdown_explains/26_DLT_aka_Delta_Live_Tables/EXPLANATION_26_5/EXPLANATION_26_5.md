# EXPLANATION 26 5

**Question 5**

**What is the primary purpose of a DLT View?**

**Correct Answer:** **C. To create temporary transformations and intermediate logic during pipeline execution**

**Explanation**

Views exist only while the pipeline runs.

They are useful for:

- intermediate joins

- filtering

- cleansing

- reusable business logic

They are **not published as permanent Delta tables.**

**Why the Correct Answer is Correct**

Views help organize complex pipelines without storing unnecessary datasets.

**Why the Other Answers Are Wrong**

Other choices incorrectly describe persistent storage.
