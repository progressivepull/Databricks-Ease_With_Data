# EXPLANATION 34 4

**Question 4**

**When is the top-down permission model most appropriate?**

**Correct Answer:** **B. When users need broad access to an entire Catalog or Schema through inherited permissions**

**Explanation**

The top-down model is ideal when a team needs access to many related objects. Administrators grant permissions once at the Catalog or Schema level, and child objects inherit those permissions automatically.

**Why the Correct Answer is Correct**

Benefits include:

- Less administrative work.

- Consistent permissions.

- Easier maintenance as new tables are created.

**Why the Other Answers Are Wrong**

The other options require many individual grants, increasing complexity.
