# EXPLANATION 34 9

**Question 9**

**What additional capabilities does the MANAGE privilege provide?**

**Correct Answer:** **C. Renaming objects, dropping objects, and changing permissions**

**Explanation**

The **MANAGE** privilege gives administrative control over an object. Users with MANAGE can:

- Rename objects.

- Drop (delete) objects.

- Grant or revoke permissions.

- Perform other administrative operations.

**Why the Correct Answer is Correct**

MANAGE is intended for administrators who need to maintain and govern Unity Catalog objects.

**Why the Other Answers Are Wrong**

The other options describe standard operational privileges rather than administrative capabilities.
