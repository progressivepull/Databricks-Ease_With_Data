# EXPLANATION 05 10

**Question 10**

**Why does the Databricks Account Console become increasingly valuable in large organizations?**

**Correct Answer:**\
✅ **It centralizes management of users, workspaces, permissions, networking, and account-wide settings.**

**Explanation**

Imagine a company with:

- 50 Workspaces

- 2,000 Users

- 100 Groups

- 500 Scheduled Jobs

- Multiple Azure subscriptions

Managing each workspace separately would be difficult.

The Account Console provides one place to manage:

- Users

- Groups

- Service Principals

- Unity Catalog

- Workspaces

- Networking

- Billing

- Serverless features

- Governance

This centralized administration improves:

- Security

- Consistency

- Compliance

- Scalability

This is why large enterprises rely heavily on the Account Console.
