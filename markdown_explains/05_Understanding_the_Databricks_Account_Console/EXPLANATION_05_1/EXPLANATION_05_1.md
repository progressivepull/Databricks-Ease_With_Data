# EXPLANATION 05 1

**Question 1**

**What is the primary purpose of the Databricks Account Console?**

**Correct Answer:**\
✅ **To centrally manage Databricks accounts, workspaces, users, and settings**

**Explanation**

The **Databricks Account Console** is the highest level of administration in Databricks.

Think of it as the **headquarters** for your entire Databricks environment.

From the Account Console, administrators can manage:

- Multiple workspaces

- Users

- Groups

- Service Principals

- Unity Catalog

- Billing

- Networking

- Account-wide settings

- Feature enablement

Unlike a workspace, which is used for day-to-day development, the Account Console manages the entire Databricks account.

**Think of it like a company**

Databricks Account\
│\
▼\
Account Console\
│\
┌──────┼──────────┐\
▼ ▼ ▼\
Workspace A Workspace B Workspace C

The Account Console manages **everything above the individual workspaces**.
