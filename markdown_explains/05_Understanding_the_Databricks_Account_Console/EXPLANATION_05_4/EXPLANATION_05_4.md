# EXPLANATION 05 4

**Question 4**

**What is the top-level container required before enabling Unity Catalog?**

**Correct Answer:**\
✅ **Metastore**

**Explanation**

The **Metastore** is the foundation of Unity Catalog.

Hierarchy:

Metastore\
│\
▼\
Catalog\
│\
▼\
Schema\
│\
▼\
Tables\
Views\
Functions\
Volumes

Without a Metastore:

- No catalogs

- No schemas

- No centralized governance

Creating the Metastore is one of the first steps in enabling Unity Catalog.
