# EXPLANATION 05 9

**Question 9**

**Which feature can be enabled at the account level through the Settings section?**

**Correct Answer:**\
✅ **Serverless Compute for Workflows, Notebooks, and Delta Live Tables (DLT)**

**Explanation**

The **Settings** section of the Account Console controls account-wide features.

Examples include:

- Serverless Compute

- Preview features

- IP Access Lists

- Account settings

Enabling **Serverless Compute** allows eligible workloads such as:

- Notebooks

- Workflows

- Delta Live Tables (DLT)

to run without users provisioning or managing clusters themselves.
