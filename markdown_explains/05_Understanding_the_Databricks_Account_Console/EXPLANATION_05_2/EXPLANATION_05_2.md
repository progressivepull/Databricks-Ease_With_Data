# EXPLANATION 05 2

**Question 2**

**Which section of the Account Console allows administrators to view and configure individual Databricks workspaces?**

**Correct Answer:**\
✅ **Workspaces**

**Explanation**

The **Workspaces** section lists every workspace in your Databricks account.

From there you can:

- View workspaces

- Create new workspaces (Account Admins)

- Delete workspaces

- Update workspace settings

- Attach Unity Catalog metastores

- Configure networking

- Assign Network Connectivity Configurations (NCCs)

Think of it as a dashboard for every Databricks workspace in your organization.
