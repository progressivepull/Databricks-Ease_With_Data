# EXPLANATION 19 10

**Question 9**

**Which of the following can be configured when scheduling a Databricks notebook as a recurring job?**

**Correct Answer: C. Frequency, widget parameters, notifications, time zone, and Cron expressions**

**Explanation**

Databricks Workflows let you automate notebook execution on a schedule.

Common scheduling options include:

- Daily

- Weekly

- Monthly

- Hourly

- Cron expressions for custom schedules

- Time zone selection

- Widget parameter values

- Email or other notifications on success or failure

For example, you can schedule a notebook to run every weekday at 6:00 AM Eastern Time with a widget value of department=Finance.

**Why is this important?**

Automation eliminates manual execution and supports reliable production data pipelines.
