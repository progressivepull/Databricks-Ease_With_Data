# EXPLANATION 19 1

**Databricks Notebook Workflows, Parameters, and DBUtils Explained (Questions 1–10)**

This lesson covers one of the most important automation concepts in Databricks: **Notebook Workflows**. Instead of writing one huge notebook, you build smaller reusable notebooks and have a **parent notebook** execute **child notebooks**, passing parameters and receiving results.

This approach is widely used in ETL pipelines, scheduled jobs, and production data engineering workflows.
