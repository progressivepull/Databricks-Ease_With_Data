# EXPLANATION 49 4

**Question 4**

**Which command verifies that the Databricks CLI has been installed successfully?**

**Correct Answer:**\
**C. databricks -v**

**Why C is Correct**

After installation, you can verify the CLI by running:

databricks -v

or

databricks --version

This displays the installed CLI version, confirming that the executable is available in your system path. ([docs.databricks.com](https://docs.databricks.com/aws/en/dev-tools/cli/install))

**Why the other choices are wrong**

Commands such as databricks login or databricks catalogs list require a working installation and, in most cases, authentication. They are not intended simply to verify installation.

**Memory Tip**

**-v = Version**
