# EXPLANATION 20 10

**Question 9**

**Why is Auto Termination considered an important cost-saving feature?**

**Correct Answer: C. It shuts down idle clusters after a configured period, reducing unnecessary cloud costs.**

**Explanation**

Interactive clusters are often left running after work is complete.

Without Auto Termination:

Notebook Closed\
↓\
\
Cluster Still Running\
↓\
\
Cloud Costs Continue

With Auto Termination:

20 Minutes Idle\
↓\
\
Cluster Stops Automatically\
↓\
\
Billing Stops

Benefits:

- Lower cloud costs.

- Prevents forgotten clusters from running overnight or over weekends.

- Especially valuable for development environments.
