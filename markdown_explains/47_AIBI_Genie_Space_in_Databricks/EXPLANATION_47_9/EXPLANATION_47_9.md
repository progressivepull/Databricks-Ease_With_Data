**Question 8**

**Which capability does Genie provide in addition to returning query results?**

**Correct Answer:**\
**B. Automatic visualizations such as bar charts, pie charts, and trend charts**

**Why B is Correct**

Genie can automatically generate visualizations that best represent the query results, including:

- bar charts

- line charts

- pie charts

- trend charts

- tables

This helps users quickly interpret results without manually creating charts.

**Why the other choices are wrong**

Genie does not automatically:

- train ML models

- deploy notebooks

- build ETL pipelines
