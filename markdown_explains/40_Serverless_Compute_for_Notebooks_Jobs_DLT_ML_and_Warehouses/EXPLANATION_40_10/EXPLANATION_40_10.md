# EXPLANATION 40 10

**Question 10**

**What happens when a Serverless Compute session becomes idle?**

**Correct Answer:**

**C. Serverless automatically terminates the idle compute session to help reduce costs.**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Why C is Correct**

Idle Serverless sessions are automatically shut down.

Benefits include:

- lower costs

- no idle clusters

- no manual termination

- efficient resource usage

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Why the others are wrong**

Idle sessions are **not**:

- kept running indefinitely

- billed forever

- waiting for manual shutdown

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Memory Tip**

Idle = Automatically Stops.
