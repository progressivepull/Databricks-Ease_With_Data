# EXPLANATION 40 7

**Question 7**

**What is the primary purpose of a Budget Policy in Serverless Compute?**

**Correct Answer:**

**C. To tag, monitor, and allocate Serverless DBU costs for reporting and chargeback**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Why C is Correct**

Budget Policies help organizations:

- track spending

- assign costs

- perform chargebacks

- organize Serverless usage by team or department

They do **not** limit compute resources directly.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Why the others are wrong**

Budget Policies do **not**:

- improve performance

- create clusters

- configure networking

- manage Unity Catalog

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Memory Tip**

Budget Policy = Cost Tracking.
