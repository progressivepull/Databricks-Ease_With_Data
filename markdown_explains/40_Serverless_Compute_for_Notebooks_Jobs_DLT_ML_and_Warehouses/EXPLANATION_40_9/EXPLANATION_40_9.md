# EXPLANATION 40 9

**Question 9**

**How is Serverless Compute billed?**

**Correct Answer:**

**C. Databricks DBUs only, with infrastructure and virtual machine costs included**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Why C is Correct**

With Classic Compute:

- Pay cloud provider for VMs.

- Pay Databricks DBUs.

With Serverless:

- Pay Databricks DBUs.

- Infrastructure costs are bundled into the Serverless pricing model, simplifying billing.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Why the others are wrong**

You do **not** separately provision or pay for VMs in your own cloud account for Serverless workloads.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Memory Tip**

Classic = VM bill + DBU.

Serverless = DBU bill (VM costs included).
