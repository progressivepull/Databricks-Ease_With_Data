# EXPLANATION 39 5

**Question 5**

**In Delta Sharing, what is the role of the Provider?**

**Correct Answer:** **C. The organization that owns the data, creates shares, and grants recipients access**

**Explanation**

The Provider is responsible for:

- Owning the data.

- Creating Shares.

- Adding tables, views, volumes, or functions to the Share.

- Granting access to Recipients.

- Managing permissions.

**Why the Correct Answer is Correct**

The Provider controls what is shared and who can access it.

**Why the Other Answers Are Wrong**

The incorrect options confuse the Provider with the Recipient or Unity Catalog.
