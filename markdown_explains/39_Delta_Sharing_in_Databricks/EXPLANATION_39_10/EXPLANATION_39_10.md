# EXPLANATION 39 10

**Question 10**

**Which Unity Catalog feature centrally manages Delta Sharing governance?**

**Correct Answer:** **C. Unity Catalog manages Providers, Recipients, Shares, permissions, and security**

**Explanation**

Unity Catalog provides centralized governance for Delta Sharing by managing:

- Providers

- Recipients

- Shares

- Shared objects

- Permissions

- Auditing

- Security policies

This central governance ensures secure and consistent data sharing across organizations.

**Why the Correct Answer is Correct**

Unity Catalog acts as the control plane for Delta Sharing, allowing administrators to manage access, permissions, and governance from one place.

**Why the Other Answers Are Wrong**

The incorrect options describe individual services or components that do not provide end-to-end governance for Delta Sharing.
