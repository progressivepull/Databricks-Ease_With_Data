# EXPLANATION 39 4

**Question 4**

**Which of the following is not one of the four primary Delta Sharing components?**

**Correct Answer:** **E. Checkpoint**

**Explanation**

The primary Delta Sharing components are:

- **Provider**

- **Share**

- **Recipient**

- **Shared Objects** (tables, views, volumes, functions)

A **checkpoint** is used in technologies like Structured Streaming and Auto Loader, not Delta Sharing.

**Why the Correct Answer is Correct**

Checkpoints track streaming progress and have no role in configuring Delta Sharing.

**Why the Other Answers Are Wrong**

The other choices are fundamental components of the Delta Sharing architecture.
