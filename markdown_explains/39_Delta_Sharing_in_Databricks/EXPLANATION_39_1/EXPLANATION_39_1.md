# EXPLANATION 39 1

**Question 1**

**What is the primary purpose of Delta Sharing in Databricks?**

**Correct Answer:** **B. To securely share live, read-only data across organizations, clouds, and platforms without duplicating the data**

**Explanation**

Delta Sharing is an **open protocol** that enables organizations to securely share **live data** with internal or external recipients without copying or moving the data. Recipients always access the latest version of the shared data, and providers maintain full control over permissions.

**Why the Correct Answer is Correct**

Delta Sharing:

- Shares live, read-only data.

- Eliminates data duplication.

- Works across organizations, cloud providers, and analytics platforms.

- Uses Unity Catalog for governance and auditing.

**Why the Other Answers Are Wrong**

- They imply copying or exporting data.

- They describe replication or migration rather than secure sharing.

- They incorrectly allow recipients to modify the provider's data.
