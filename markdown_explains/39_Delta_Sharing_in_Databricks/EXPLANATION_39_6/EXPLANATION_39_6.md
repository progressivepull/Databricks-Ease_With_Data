# EXPLANATION 39 6

**Question 6**

**What information is required to create a Databricks-to-Databricks recipient?**

**Correct Answer:** **C. Recipient name, sharing method, and the recipient workspace's Sharing Identifier**

**Explanation**

When configuring Databricks-to-Databricks sharing, the provider needs:

- A recipient name.

- The Databricks sharing method.

- The recipient's **Sharing Identifier**, which uniquely identifies the recipient workspace for secure sharing.

**Why the Correct Answer is Correct**

These details allow Unity Catalog to establish a secure trust relationship between provider and recipient.

**Why the Other Answers Are Wrong**

The incorrect options omit required information or include unnecessary details.
