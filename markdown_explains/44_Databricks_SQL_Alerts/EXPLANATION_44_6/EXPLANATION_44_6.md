# EXPLANATION 44 6

**Question 6**

**If a SQL Alert is configured with the condition Pending Orders \> 10,000 and the query returns 191,189, what will happen?**

**Correct Answer:**\
**C. The alert status becomes Triggered because the condition evaluates to TRUE.**

**Why C is Correct**

Evaluate the condition:

191,189 \> 10,000

TRUE

Since the expression evaluates to **TRUE**, the alert status becomes **Triggered**, and configured notifications are sent according to the alert's notification settings.

**Why the other choices are wrong**

The alert will not remain OK or ignore the result because the threshold has clearly been exceeded.

**Memory Tip**
