# EXPLANATION 44 9

**Question 9**

**Who can create notification destinations such as Email, Slack, or Microsoft Teams in Databricks?**

**Correct Answer:**\
**C. Workspace Administrators**

**Why C is Correct**

Workspace Administrators manage system-wide notification destinations.

They configure:

- Email destinations

- Slack integrations

- Microsoft Teams integrations

- Webhook destinations

Regular users can subscribe to available destinations but typically cannot create new system destinations.

**Why the other choices are wrong**

Standard users and analysts generally do not have permission to create workspace-wide notification destinations.

**Memory Tip**

**Admins create. Users subscribe.**
