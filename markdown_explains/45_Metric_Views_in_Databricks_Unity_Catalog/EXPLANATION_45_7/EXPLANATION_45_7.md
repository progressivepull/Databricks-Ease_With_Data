# EXPLANATION 45 7

**Question 7**

**What advanced capability allows Metric Views to calculate values such as Current Year Orders and Previous Year Orders?**

**Correct Answer:**\
**B. Window-based measures**

**Why B is Correct**

Window-based measures support time-aware calculations such as:

- Current Year Sales

- Previous Year Sales

- Year-over-Year Growth

- Running Totals

- Rolling 12 Months

These calculations are defined once and reused consistently.

**Why the other choices are wrong**

Basic measures cannot express these advanced analytical calculations by themselves.
