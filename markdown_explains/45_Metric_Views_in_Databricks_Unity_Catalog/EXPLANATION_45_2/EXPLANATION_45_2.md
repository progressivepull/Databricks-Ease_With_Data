# EXPLANATION 45 2

**Question 2**

**Which two types of objects are defined within a Metric View?**

**Correct Answer:**\
**C. Dimensions and Measures**

**Why C is Correct**

Every Metric View contains two primary business concepts:

**Dimensions**

Fields used for grouping and filtering.

Examples:

- Customer

- Product

- Region

- Date

- Salesperson

**Measures**

Business calculations.

Examples:

- Revenue

- Total Sales

- Order Count

- Average Price

- Profit

Together they create the semantic model.

**Why the other choices are wrong**

Tables, notebooks, dashboards, and SQL Warehouses are not the two core modeling objects inside a Metric View.

**Memory Tip**

**Dimension = Describe**

**Measure = Calculate**
