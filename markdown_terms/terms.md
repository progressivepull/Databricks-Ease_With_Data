# Terms

* ## Snowflake

In the tech industry, Snowflake refers to Snowflake Inc., a major cloud-based data platform.

	- The AI Data Cloud: It offers a unified platform for data warehousing, data lakes, data engineering, and data sharing across multiple public clouds like AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud.
	
	- Core Architecture: Its main technical advantage is the separation of compute and storage resources, which allows businesses to scale up or down instantly and only pay for what they use.
	
	- AI Capabilities: Through features like Snowflake Cortex AI and the Snowflake CoWork personal work agent, enterprises can build and run generative AI apps directly within their secure data perimeter.
