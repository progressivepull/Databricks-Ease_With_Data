Question 1
What is one of the biggest advantages of Delta Live Tables (DLT) regarding pipeline datasets?
A. Developers must manually create and delete every Delta table.
B. DLT automatically manages the lifecycle of datasets created within a pipeline.
C. DLT stores all datasets only in local notebook memory.
D. Developers must manually register every table in Unity Catalog.
E. DLT automatically converts all tables into streaming tables.
Correct Answer: B. DLT automatically manages the lifecycle of datasets created within a pipeline.
