Question 1
Why should sensitive information such as passwords and API keys be stored in Secret Scopes instead of hardcoded in notebooks?
A. Hardcoded credentials improve notebook performance.
B. Secret Scopes automatically encrypt all Delta tables.
C. Secret Scopes provide centralized, secure credential management and prevent accidental exposure.
D. Hardcoded credentials are required for Databricks Jobs.
E. Secret Scopes eliminate the need for authentication.
Correct Answer: C. Secret Scopes provide centralized, secure credential management and prevent accidental exposure.
