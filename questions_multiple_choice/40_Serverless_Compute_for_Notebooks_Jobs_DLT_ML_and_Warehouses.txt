Question 1
What is the primary purpose of Databricks Serverless Compute?
A. To require users to manually configure Spark clusters before running code
B. To automatically manage compute infrastructure, including cluster creation, scaling, and termination
C. To replace Unity Catalog with a new governance system
D. To permanently store Delta Lake tables
E. To eliminate the need for Databricks SQL Warehouses
Correct Answer: B. To automatically manage compute infrastructure, including cluster creation, scaling, and termination

How does the Serverless Compute architecture differ from Classic Compute?
