Question 1
What is the primary purpose of Databricks Auto Loader?
A. To create Delta Lake tables from SQL scripts only
B. To continuously optimize Delta tables for performance
C. To incrementally ingest new files from cloud storage into Delta Lake tables
D. To replace Structured Streaming with batch processing
E. To convert Parquet files into CSV files automatically
Correct Answer: C. To incrementally ingest new files from cloud storage into Delta Lake tables
