Question 1
What is the primary purpose of Delta Live Tables (DLT) in Databricks?
A. To manually configure Spark clusters for every ETL job
B. To simplify ETL development by automatically managing pipeline operations while developers define transformations
C. To replace Delta Lake with Parquet files
D. To create dashboards directly from raw data
E. To manage Azure storage accounts only
Correct Answer: B. To simplify ETL development by automatically managing pipeline operations while developers define transformations
