Question 1
What is the primary purpose of Lakehouse Federation in Databricks?
A. To automatically copy external databases into Delta Lake
B. To query external data sources directly without moving the data into the Lakehouse
C. To convert relational databases into Unity Catalog metastores
D. To replace SQL Warehouses with Spark clusters
E. To automatically back up external databases into cloud storage
Correct Answer: B. To query external data sources directly without moving the data into the Lakehouse
